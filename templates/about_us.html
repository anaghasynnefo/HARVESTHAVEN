Who We Are?

You must have heard the saying "This or that whatever we befall, the farmer must feed them all!" Countless superheros, hace been born on this earth, while many are fictional, a handful are real. Well we represent the real superheros- The Farmers Harvest Haven aims at unlocking access to safe-to-eat,pesticide screened fruits and vegetables directly sourced from our farmers. We are an online platform available to you on both website and mobile application. At Harvest Haven we are in the quest to create a positive movement- a movement aimed at reducing the gap between the consumer & a farmer, the movement that gives consumers to access to safe and non toxic farmers produce a movement that also uplifts the farmer community. Harvest Haven is agri tech start up bridging the gap between the fruits and vegetables supply chain. We connect the rural farmers to urban consumers.
